Important FAQ

01. Can anyone become an affiliate for Genting Vegas?

Anyone can apply, however, there are a number of criteria which must be met in order for an application to be successful.

02. Is it possible to be an affiliate of other affiliate programs with competitors?

Yes, Genting Vegas does not restrict its affiliates in this regard.

03. Can I keep track of my earnings?

You can log into your affiliate account to view a summary of your earnings.

04. How often are my affiliate statistics updated?

Affiliate statistics are updated everyday

05. Is there a minimum earning before payout?

The minimum earning before payout is 60 RM. Please send an email to affiliateteam@Gentingvegas.com should you wish to enquire about possible lower earnings.

06. How many of my websites can I register?

This is solely at your discretion. You will receive individual tracking links for each site so that you can see how each is performing individually.

07. Can I lose money on this?

No, there’s no negative carry-over and we never charge for a negative commission during a slow month (e.g. if one of your players hits the jackpot).

08. How does Genting Vegas keep track of the customers I referred?

Once you’re aboard, you can log into the affiliate program where you will have access to our marketing materials such as banners, text links, etc. All of this material will have a tracking code connected to your affiliate account. With this code, we are able to keep track of all the revenue you have acquired.

09. Why should you join Genting Vegas Affiliates?

Genting Vegas has gained a reputation for creating a work ethic which aims at attaining a common goal for all involved. Being a part of Genting Vegas means working together so as to win together.

10. Do I need to send an invoice?

This depends on your payment method option. There is no need to send an invoice if you have chosen 'Neteller', 'Skrill' or 'Wire Transfer' option. This is because you will get paid automatically by the 15th of each month. However, if your bank account is based outside of the MALAYSIA, or you do not have an IBAN, unfortunately, the automated wire transfer option will not be available to you. In this case, please set your method to ‘Cheque’ and send an invoice to invoice@Gentingvegas.com monthly.

11. How will I get paid?

This will depend on how you set up your affiliate account. You can choose between Wire Transfer, Neteller or Skrill. Once your details have been updated in the system, your next payment will be sent before the 15th of each following month (as stated in our T&C’s). In the case of Wire Transfer method, you will receive a payment reference statement including your affiliate ID and corresponding payment period. There is no need to send us an invoice each month if your affiliate account is up to date with a valid IBAN and BIC/SWIFT. If selecting the Wire Transfer option, you must ensure that your chosen bank account accepts payments in your currency.
